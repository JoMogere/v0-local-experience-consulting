# BookedUp Africa - 8 Pillar Content Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive SEO content strategy for BookedUp Africa, built on 8 major content pillars covering the full spectrum of hotel marketing and growth strategies.

## Strategy Goals

- **Topical Authority**: Position BookedUp Africa as the comprehensive resource for hospitality marketing in Africa
- **SEO Dominance**: Rank for primary keywords across 8 major topics + supporting long-tail keywords
- **Lead Generation**: Drive qualified hotel owner traffic through high-intent content
- **EEAT Signals**: Build expertise, authoritativeness, trustworthiness, and experience signals for Google

## Content Structure Per Pillar

Each pillar page includes:

### 1. **Comprehensive Coverage**
- 4,000-6,000 word depth (pillar pages)
- 1,500-2,500 words (supporting articles)
- Covers all reasonable questions about the topic

### 2. **EEAT Signals**
- ✓ Original insights from our hospitality marketing work
- ✓ Real examples and mini case studies
- ✓ Statistics with sources
- ✓ Practical checklists and action items
- ✓ Visual diagrams and screenshots
- ✓ FAQs with 10-15 questions
- ✓ Clear calls to action

### 3. **Technical SEO**
- ✓ Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- ✓ Table of contents for easy navigation
- ✓ Internal links to related articles
- ✓ Schema markup (Article, FAQ, BreadcrumbList)
- ✓ Optimized meta titles and descriptions
- ✓ Mobile-first responsive design

### 4. **Internal Linking Strategy**
```
Hotel Marketing & Growth
├── Local SEO (links to GBP, Direct Bookings)
├── Google Business Profile (links to Local SEO, Reviews)
├── Experiential Marketing (links to Content Marketing, Case Studies)
├── Content Marketing (links to Direct Bookings, Website Design)
├── Direct Bookings (links to Website Design, Local SEO)
├── Hotel Websites (links to Content Marketing, Schema)
├── AI for Hotels (links to Content Marketing, Direct Bookings)
└── Case Studies (links to all pillars)
```
